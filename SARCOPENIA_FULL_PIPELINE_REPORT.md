## Stage 6: Final Recommendations

### 🥇 #1: Rapamycin (Rapamycin)
- **Target**: MTOR (mTORC1)
- **Score**: 92/100
- **Evidence**: ITP study shows 25% lifespan extension in mice
- **Clinical status**: Approved (Sirolimus), off-label for longevity
- **Rationale**: Most validated longevity compound, multiple mechanisms
- **Concerns**: Immunosuppression at high doses

### 🥈 #2: Bimagrumab (BYM357)
- **Target**: Myostatin (MSTN)
- **Score**: 88/100
- **Evidence**: Phase 2 shows +5% muscle mass in sarcopenia patients
- **Clinical status**: Phase 2 for inclusion body myositis
- **Rationale**: Directly targets muscle growth pathway
- **Advantage**: Antibody-based, better safety profile

### 🥉 #3: Metformin
- **Target**: AMPK
- **Score**: 85/100
- **Evidence**: TAME trial targeting aging as indication
- **Clinical status**: Approved, excellent safety profile
- **Rationale**: Cheap, safe, longevity signals in diabetic patients
- **Advantage**: Already widely prescribed

### 4️⃣ #4: Urolithin A
- **Target**: Mitophagy (mitochondria)
- **Score**: 80/100
- **Evidence**: Human trial shows improved muscle function
- **Clinical status**: Dietary supplement (Mitopure™)
- **Rationale**: Targets aging mitochondria, gut-brain-muscle axis
- **Advantage**: Natural metabolite, no safety concerns

### 5️⃣ #5: Resveratrol
- **Target**: SIRT1
- **Score**: 78/100
- **Evidence**: Mixed results in human trials
- **Clinical status**: Dietary supplement
- **Rationale**: Calorie restriction mimetic, synergizes with exercise
- **Concerns**: Low bioavailability
