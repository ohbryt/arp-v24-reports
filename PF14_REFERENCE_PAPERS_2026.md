## 🔑 Core PF14 Papers (Must-Read)

### 1. Lung-Targeted RNAi In Vivo (KEY REFERENCE)
```
Title: Effective lung-targeted RNAi in mice with peptide-based delivery of nucleic acid
Journal: Scientific Reports (2019)
DOI: 10.1038/s41598-019-56455-2
PMID: PMC6934651

Key findings:
• PF14 and NF55 efficiently deliver siRNA to lungs IN VIVO
• Both nucleic acid types (siRNA and plasmid) effective
• Reduced lung inflammation in mice
• Lung enrichment: 16- to 350-fold over non-lung tissues
• No obvious histological injury

CRITICAL for our protocol:
- This is THE in vivo lung delivery paper for PF14
- N/P ratio and formulation details included
```

### 2. PF14 Mechanism & Characterization
```
Title: PepFect 14, a novel cell-penetrating peptide for oligonucleotide delivery
Journal: PMC (2011)
PMID: PMC3130259

Key findings:
• Stearic acid modification at N-terminus
• Low cytotoxicity
• Forms non-covalent complexes with oligonucleotides
• High transfection efficiency
• Independent of cell confluency
```

### 3. mRNA Delivery In Vivo
```
Title: PepFect14 mediates the delivery of mRNA into human primary keratinocytes and in vivo
Journal: Frontiers in Pharmacology (2023)
DOI: 10.3389/fphar.2023.1219761
PMID: PMC10374019

Key findings:
• PF14 delivers mRNA effectively
• Works with various nucleic acids: pDNA, siRNA, miRNA, antisense
• Low cytotoxicity
• Can be used with additives (MgCl2, chloroquine, PS80)
• Used in ovarian cancer mouse model (van den Brand et al., 2019)
```

### 4. hES Cell Transfection
```
Title: Targeted gene silencing in human embryonic stem cells using PepFect 14
Journal: Stem Cell Research & Therapy (2019)
DOI: 10.1186/s13287-019-1144-x

Key findings:
• Effective siRNA delivery to hES cells
• ROCK inhibitor containing medium improves transfection
• Low cytotoxicity in stem cells
```

### 5. Stearic Acid Modification
```
Title: Applications of cell penetrating peptide-based drug delivery
Journal: Frontiers in Immunology (2025)
DOI: 10.3389/fimmu.2025.1540192

Key findings:
• Stearic acid (stearyl) modification increases transfection
• Ornithine residues reduce serum protease susceptibility
• PF14 derived from transportan 10 (TP10)
• Comparison with other PepFect peptides
```

Buffer: MQ-water or indicated media
N/P ratio: N/P 2 in vivo
