## 2. Strategy 1: Inhalation Delivery (мөңкі  мҡ°м„ )

### Why Inhalation?

```
INHALATION ADVANTAGES:

1. DIRECT TO LUNG
   в””в”Җв”Җ siRNA reaches lung tumor directly
   в””в”Җв”Җ Minimal systemic exposure
   в””в”Җв”Җ Bypasses GI tract entirely

2. LOCAL CONCENTRATION
   в””в”Җв”Җ High dose in lung
   в””в”Җв”Җ Low dose in other organs

3. APPROVED TECHNOLOGY
   в””в”Җв”Җ Inhalation insulin (Afrezza)
   в””в”Җв”Җ Inhalation RNA (COVID vaccines)
   в””в”Җв”Җ Clinical precedent exists
```

### Delivery Vehicle Options

| Vehicle | Pros | Cons | Status |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **Lipid nanoparticles (LNPs)** | Clinical, tunable | Needs optimization for lung | Approved (COVID) |
| **Exosomes** | Natural, low immunogenicity | Variable batch, scalable | Preclinical |
| **Polymer nanoparticles** | Tunable, stable | Potential toxicity | Preclinical |
| **Microspheres** | Sustained release | Large particles | Approved |

### Inhalation Device Options

| Device | Type | Company | Status |
|--------|------|---------|--------|
| **Nebulizer** | Mesh/jet | Various | Clinical |
| **Dry powder inhaler** | DPI | Various | Clinical |
| **Soft mist inhaler** | Respimat | Boehringer | Clinical |

### Proposed Formulation

```
DGAT1 siRNA-LUNG-NP (Nanoparticle)

Composition:
в”ңв”Җв”Җ siRNA (DGAT1-targeting) : 20-50 Ојg per dose
в”ңв”Җв”Җ Ionizable lipid (MC3)   : 50 mol%
в”ңв”Җв”Җ DSPC (phospholipid)     : 10 mol%
в”ңв”Җв”Җ Cholesterol              : 38.5 mol%
в”ңв”Җв”Җ PEG-lipid                : 1.5 mol%
в””в”Җв”Җ Lung-targeting ligand    : (optional) RGD peptide

Physical properties:
в”ңв”Җв”Җ Particle size: 80-150 nm
в”ңв”Җв”Җ Zeta potential: Near neutral
в”ңв”Җв”Җ PdI: < 0.2
в””в”Җв”Җ Dose: 1-2 mg siRNA/day

Administration:
в””в”Җв”Җ Inhalation via nebulizer (2-3 min)
```
