# Literature Alert: SEE-CITE Chemoproteomics
## Small-Molecule Binding Site Discovery | Nature Chemistry

**Date:** 2026-04-27 (Published)  
**DOI:** 10.1038/s41557-026-02127-4  
**Authors:** Chau Ngo, Sho Takechi, Aditya Sivakumar, Miranda Villanueva, Fengchao Yu et al.

---

## Key Innovation

```
SEE-CITE: Silyl Ether Enables Chemoproteomic Interaction and Target Engagement

Method:
- Cleavable silyl ether linkages in PAL (photoaffinity labeling) probes
- Pinpoint precise binding sites
- Quantify target engagement

Advantage:
- High coverage interaction site mapping
- Site-of-labeling identification
- Head-to-head comparisons
```

---

## Core Concept

```
Small molecule binding site discovery:
├── Traditional: Low coverage
├── Problem: Can't pinpoint exact site
└── SEE-CITE solution: Cleavable linker + proteomics

Key breakthrough:
- Fully functionalized chemically cleavable crosslinking handle
- Precise site-of-labeling identification
- Quantitative comparison between probes
```
